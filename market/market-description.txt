Have your Worldcoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Worldcoin Wallet is the first mobile Worldcoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Worldcoin amount in WDC and wWDC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Worldcoin via NFC, QR-codes or Worldcoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Worldcoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Worldcoin balance.

You can find more about Worldcoin at
http://worldcoinalliance.net

If you want to contribute to Worldcoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/worldcoinproject/android-worldcoin-wallet
